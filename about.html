Why you should start with my page when first trying Vietnamese food

Hello everyone, I am Anna and I am from Vietnam. Vietnam is a beautiful country with many delicious dishes (other than the famous Pho!). This is a small blog of recommended Vietnamese dishes from my personal opinions for everyone!

Cuisines packed with abundant and healthy nutritious value

For Vietnamese food, you can always find nutritious dishes that match your taste. Whether you are vegan or non-vegan, spicy eater or non-spicy eater, you can always enjoy the food here without being worried about nutrition value.

That is why the rate of obesity in Vietnam is incredible low comparing to many other countries!

Spice your life up with Vietnamese cuisine! Suitable for beginners who are not used to too much spices!

Vietnamese cuisines is a wonderland for any eater. Vietnameses have all kinds of tastes, whether fresh, salty, sweet, or spicy. But there is one thing I can promise is that you need a little spice in your life!

An amazing variety of vegan dishes

Live healthier. Protect the environment.

Although Vietnamese cuisines are famous with meat-included dishes, I want to give more on the vegan side because it is a huge but little to be known culture. I personally do not like vegetables and greens, however, I just love the vegan dishes here.

You do not have to commit to any side, just simply enjoy the food!
